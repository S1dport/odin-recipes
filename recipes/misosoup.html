Miso Soup

Miso soup is a traditional Japanese soup consisting of a dashi stock into
which softened miso paste is mixed. There are many optional ingredients
that may be added depending on personal preference.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tsp dashi granules
  • 3 tbsp miso paste
  • 8oz package silken tofu, diced
  • 2 green onions, sliced into small pieces
  1. Combine water and dashi granules in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat; bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat to medium and whisk in miso paste. Stir in tofu.
  3. Separate the layers of green onions, and add them to the soup.
  4. Simmer gently for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
